Kitchen Calm
Our Mission
Kitchen calm addresses the unique challenges faced by individuals with ADHD in meal preparation by offering a specialized online resource with easy-to-follow and delicious recipes. The thoughtfully crafted recipes prioritize simplicity, breaking down each step for an enjoyable and stress-free cooking experience. To enhance accessibility, step-by-step visual guides are included, recognizing the significance of visual cues for individuals with ADHD.
